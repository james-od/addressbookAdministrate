This is a small web application that models an addressbook. Contacts and organisations can be added and viewed. To add a contact you must first ensure the organisation they are in has already been added. The contact can then be added under the same organisation name.
I'm trying to focus on fulfilling the requirements exactly. Although I feel I have met them there a several flaws in my design and implementation. As the recommended time was only four hours I thought it best to leave these flaws provided the requirements were met.
